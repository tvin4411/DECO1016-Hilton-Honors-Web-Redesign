Membership Tier

Greater rewards awaits you. See the different tiers you can access when you meet the requirements

The highest tier is available to members who either:

  • have 30 stays or spent 60 nights in Hilton properties in a calendar year
  • earned 120,000 Honors Base Points in a year.

Benefits include the following:

  • A 50% point bonus
  • A guaranteed room when you make a reservation at least 48 hours prior to your arrival
  • Spa, golf or restaurant discounts
  • Complimentary meals and snacks
  • Point bonuses for stays at participating Hilton Worldwide properties

You’ll achieve Gold status when you either:

  • had 20 stays or spend 40 nights in Hilton properties.
  • earned 75,000 Honors Base Points in a calendar year.

In addition to the benefits available to Blue and Silver members, you can get:

  • A 25% point bonus
  • Upgrades to a preferred room
  • Complimentary breakfasts
  • Complimentary snacks
  • Point bonuses for stays at participating Hilton properties

You’ll make it to the second level when you have had 4 stays or spent 10 nights in a Hilton property within a calendar year.


This provides you with:

  • A 15% point bonus on the base rate.
  • A free night for Standard Room Reward stays of 5 nights or more.
  • A “Second Guest Stays Free” offer.
  • Complimentary access to fitness centers
  • Two complimentary bottles of water per stay at participating hotels and resorts

The base level of the memberships you can obtain and it offers the following benefits:

  • Points for eligible spending with program partners.
  • A guaranteed booking discount.
  • Digital check-in.
  • Complimentary in-room and lobby standard Internet access at participating Hilton Worldwide properties.
  • The ability to register your room preferences for faster bookings and check-in.
  • Late check-out at participating Hilton Worldwide properties
  • Express check-out.
  • A “Second Guest Stays Free” offer at participating Hilton Worldwide properties.
